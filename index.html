The Problem: Why Quitting is So Hard

Quitting smoking may seem difficult or impossible—but it’s not. Traditional quitting methods have poor success rates, with doctor's advice working for only 2 out of 100 people and nicotine gums or patches working for just 6 to 8 out of 100 people.

The truth is that willpower alone fails 96% of the time because addiction isn't logical; it's psychological.

The Solution: A Smarter Way to Quit

The Smoke Break program from Smokasha doesn't fight the habit; it transforms the root desire to smoke. It is based on neuroplasticity, your brain's ability to change and adapt.

We use proven techniques like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Mindfulness, and Trigger Management. These methods have much higher success rates because they address the psychological addiction that nicotine substitutes often don't.

My Quitting Journey

How Smoke Break from Smokasha Works

The program was created by an ex-smoker and has been successfully tested on 72 individuals. It features interactive daily modules, immersive exercises, and personal coaching.

Our philosophy is "Positivity over Guilt," creating a supportive and encouraging environment to help you become a happy non-smoker, not a deprived one. The program is for all nicotine-based products and should not be combined with other techniques.
